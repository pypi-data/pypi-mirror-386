Program Idem

Usage

Idem file [-d] [-o]
file: bravais_TYP containing generators of a finite unimodular group.

Description

Calculates the central primitive idempotents of the rational commuting (or equivalently envelloping) algebra of the group in file. (This is used for splitting the natural module Q^n into homogeneous components.)

Options

 -d     : Outputs the dimensions of the commuting algebra and its centre.
 -o     : Gives a Z-basis of the commuting algebra and its centre.

Remarks

CAUTION: The program might give wrong answers for degrees bigger than 6!
