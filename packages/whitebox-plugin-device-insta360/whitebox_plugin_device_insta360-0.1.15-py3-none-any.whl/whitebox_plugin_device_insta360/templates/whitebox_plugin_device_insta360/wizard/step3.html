Camera: Activate

To activate your camera, power it on and enable Wi-Fi in settings (Settings > Wi-Fi Settings).

Then, download the Insta360 app from the App Store on iPhones, or Insta360 website on Androids. Follow the app’s prompts to connect to your camera’s Wi-Fi and complete the pairing process. The app will also guide you through any necessary firmware updates.
